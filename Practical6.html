Darshan Raval - A Tribute

Darshan Raval, Indian Singer
About Darshan Raval

Darshan Raval is an Indian singer, composer, and songwriter known for his soulful voice. He gained fame as the 1st runner-up of India’s Raw Star (2014) and has since become one of the leading voices in Bollywood and independent music.

Personal Information
  • Full Name: Darshan Raval
  • Born: October 18, 1994 (Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India)
  • Height: 5'8"
  • Eye Color: Black
  • Hobbies: Playing Guitar, Traveling, Composing Music
Career Journey

Early Life & Entry into Music

Passionate about music since childhood, Darshan chose music over engineering, which his father initially wanted him to pursue. His journey began in 2014 when he gained national recognition in India’s Raw Star.

Bollywood & Independent Hits

  • Bollywood debut with "Jab Tum Chaho" from *Prem Ratan Dhan Payo* (2015).
  • Independent hits like "Tera Zikr" (2017) and "Ek Tarfa" (2020).
  • Worked on popular Bollywood songs such as "Chogada" (Loveyatri) and "Kamariya" (Mitron).
  • Released his debut album "Judaiyaan" (2020), followed by "Dard" (2023).
Achievements
  • Winner of multiple music awards for his Bollywood and independent songs.
  • Millions of followers on YouTube, Instagram, and streaming platforms.
  • Recognized as a youth icon for his deep and emotional lyrics.
Why Fans Love Him

I’d say Darshan Raval’s voice carries an emotional depth that touches the soul. He expresses love, pain, and joy in a way that truly connects with fans, making him a standout in the Indian music scene.
