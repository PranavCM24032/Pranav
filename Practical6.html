Darshan Raval, One of the legendary Indian singers
About Him

Darshan Raval is a famous Indian singer and songwriter. He rose to fame in 2014 through the show India's Raw Star. His unique voice and emotional songs have won hearts across India.

Personal Details
  • Full Name: Darshan Raval
  • Date of Birth: October 18, 1994
  • Birthplace: Ahmedabad, Gujarat
  • Hobbies: Guitar, Composing Music, Traveling

Career Highlights

  • Albums: Judaiyaan (2020), Dard (2023)
Why Fans Love Him

Darshan's music touches hearts. His songs are full of emotion, and his humble personality makes him very relatable. He connects with fans through both his music and his kindness.
